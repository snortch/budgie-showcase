🦜 Budgie

The budget that says GO.

A weekly plan. A squishy pie. One small bird that tells you the truth about your money before you spend it.

PLAN

A budget you can squish.

Your money is a soft 3D pie. Bills, savings, and spendable, split in one view. The bird reads it out in one line: bills covered, money set aside, $1,190 to spend. Tap a wedge to split it further.

FIT

Add a thing. Budgie makes room.

Every category is a wedge you can see and squeeze. Add an espresso habit and the pie grows a wedge on the spot. If the week goes over, Budgie asks you to trim a little. Nothing hides in a spreadsheet row.

ASK

Ask Budgie anything about your money.

Ask is Budgie’s built-in AI. Type any money question. How am I doing? What’s safe this week? Can Fun cover what Food went over? Budgie answers, then takes care of it. It works out the fix and sets everything up for you. Nothing moves until you say Go.

INCOME

Paychecks drift. Your plan keeps up.

Budgie notices when your income changes shape. Say $1,450 landed the last two times instead of the usual $1,300. The fix is one green button. Update the number, keep your plan the same.

SORT

Every charge finds a home in seconds.

New spending lines up with Budgie’s best guess already attached. A fuel-stop charge probably belongs in Gas. One tap files it. Budgie learns your rules for next time.

GUIDE

Say what you’re doing. Budgie walks you through it.

No settings maze. Five plain doors: rebalance my spending, my income changed, manage categories, review my plan, start over. Pick one and the bird handles the arithmetic.

TOGETHER

Save as a duet.

Pair up with a friend for a streak. Your half shows week-by-week progress. Their half is just a bar. You share the goal, not your bank account.

WIN

You closed it together.

Finish a duet and both flocks earn the moment. Party Hat Budgie, new for both of you. Money habits stick better when someone else is on the ring.

FLOCK

Every win hatches a bird.

Awards live in a nest, not a trophy case. Keep your streaks and the eggs crack open. Cozy Beanie. Nightcap. Little Sprout. Eighteen budgies to hatch, one good week at a time.
