🦜 Budgie

The budget that says GO.

A weekly plan, a squishy pie, and one small bird who tells you the truth about your money — before you spend it, not after.

GLANCE

One word when you open it.

Home answers the only question that matters — am I okay to spend? — with a GO you can read from across the room. The week bar shows your pace; when something needs attention, Budgie says it plainly: “Going a bit fast — worth easing up.”

PLAN

A budget you can squish.

Your week is a soft 3D pie — every category a wedge you can actually see. Add “Espresso” and Budgie makes room honestly: “you’re $542/wk over now. Trim a little to make room for it.” Nothing hides in a spreadsheet row.

ASK

Ask Budgie anything about your money.

Ask is Budgie’s built-in AI. Type any question — “How am I doing?”, “What’s safe this week?”, “Food went over — can Fun cover it?” — and Budgie doesn’t just answer, it takes care of it: works out the fix, sets it up for you, and handles the follow-through. You get a plain-English answer and a ready-to-go plan, nothing moves until you say Go.

INCOME

Paychecks drift. Your plan keeps up.

Budgie notices when your income changes shape — “$1,450 landed the last two times — it was $1,300 before” — and offers the fix as one green button: update the number, keep your plan the same.

SORT

Every charge finds a home in seconds.

New spending lines up with Budgie’s best guess already attached: “similar to J&M Fuel Stop — that’s in Gas.” One tap each, and Budgie learns your rules for next time.

GUIDE

Say what you’re doing. Budgie walks you through it.

No settings maze. Five plain doors — rebalance my spending, my income changed, manage categories, review my plan, start over — and a bird who handles the arithmetic behind each one.

TOGETHER

Save as a duet.

Pair up with a friend for a streak — your half is week-by-week pips, their half is just a progress bar. “No amounts, no week-by-week”: you share the goal, not your bank account.

WIN

You closed it together.

Finish a duet and both flocks earn the moment — Party Hat Budgie, new for both of you. Money habits are easier when someone else is on the ring.

FLOCK

Every win hatches a bird.

Awards live in a nest, not a trophy case. Keep your streaks and the eggs crack open — Cozy Beanie, Nightcap, Little Sprout — 18 budgies to hatch, each earned one good week at a time.
